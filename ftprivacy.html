1. Personal Information Collection and Use

If you grant Foodie Thing permission, we may collect and use personal information from you as follows.

  • Camera
    Purpose: Foodie Thing needs access to the camera to enable AR functionality

  • Photos
    Purpose: Foodie Thing needs to export AR Media Foodie Thing wants to add the AR pictures you take to your photo library

Please note that you can decline a permission or revoke it after you granted it in the iOS settings. If you do so, we will not collect your personal information related to that permission. However, the functionality of Foodie Thing may be limited as a result.

We may receive technical information about your device, for example, its IP address or operating system.


2. Personal Information Sharing

Foodie Thing integrates the following third party services that are not requesting any permission:

  • Firebase
  • GoogleAppMeasurement

All third party services may receive technical information about your device, for example, its IP address or operating system version. Also, they may keep your personal information over time and across different apps and websites.


3. Tracking Technologies

Neither Foodie Thing nor any third party services are using tracking technologies.

4. Social Logins

Foodie Thing does not use any social login functionality.


5. In-app Purchase Information

Foodie Thing does not process any in-app purchase information.


6. Children's Personal Information

Users of Foodie Thing must be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take all reasonable steps to delete it as soon as possible. If you become aware that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the contact information below.

7. How Long We Keep Your Personal Information

We will keep the personal information that we collect as long as it is necessary for the purposes for which we collect it.


8. How We Protect Your Personal Information

We use encryption to protect personal information stored and transmitted online. Only personnel who need the personal information to perform a specific function have access to such. The computers and servers on which we store personal information are kept in a secure environment.


9. Policy Changes

Privacy Policy changes will be emailed to every user registered in the Foodie Thing Network. Any user who does not agree to the changes should not continue to use the Foodie Thing app or be a part of the Foodie Thing Network. Continued usage of these services signifies acceptance of the policy changes.


10. Accessibility

This website conforms to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. However, if you are having difficulties obtaining information from it, please contact us at the contact information below. We will try to make the information available to you in another format and answer any question that you may have.
