About ColorCracker

ColorCracker is an addictive daily puzzle game that challenges your color perception and hexadecimal knowledge. Crack the code of colors and test your RGB skills!

How to Play

  • Face a new color challenge every day
  • Guess the correct hexadecimal code in 5 attempts or less
  • Receive feedback on each digit: too high, too low, or spot on
  • Sharpen your skills and return daily for a fresh challenge

Key Features

  • Daily color puzzles that reset at midnight
  • Intuitive feedback system to guide your guesses
  • Track your progress and compete with friends via Game Center
  • Clean, colorful interface that's easy on the eyes

Perfect for:

  • Design enthusiasts
  • Developers
  • Puzzle lovers
  • Anyone looking to improve their color perception

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: When does the daily challenge reset?
    A: The challenge resets every day at midnight local time.
  • Q: How do I interpret the feedback on my guesses?
    A: For each digit in your guess, you'll see if it's too high, too low, or correct.
  • Q: Can I play more than one challenge per day?
    A: ColorCracker offers one challenge per day to keep things fresh and exciting.
