1. Personal Information Collection and Use

If you grant BeautySpot permission, we may collect and use personal information from you as follows.

  • Location Services
    Purpose: BeautySpot needs your location to show you businesses in your area. This is the core functionality of the app.

  • Camera
    Purpose: For uploading a cover photo

  • Photos
    Purpose: BeautySpot allows you to upload a photo from your library.

Please note that you can decline a permission or revoke it after you granted it in the iOS settings. If you do so, we will not collect your personal information related to that permission. However, the functionality of BeautySpot may be limited as a result.

We may receive technical information about your device, for example, its IP address or operating system.

6. Children's Personal Information

If you become aware that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the contact information below.

7. How Long We Keep Your Personal Information

We will keep the personal information that we collect as long as the BeautySpot platform is running. The owner of the information may request that they be removed from the BeautySpot platform, in which case, the moderators of BeautySpot, including but not limited to: the developers and managers, will be willing to comply.


8. How We Protect Your Personal Information

We use encryption to protect personal information stored and transmitted online. Only personnel who need the personal information to perform a specific function have access to such. The computers and servers on which we store personal information are kept in a secure environment. We do not collect any sensitive personal data. In the event of a data breach, all users of the BeautySpot platform will be notified immediately via email.


9. Policy Changes

When we make material changes to this privacy policy, for example, due to app updates or changes in the law, we will email the updated policy to all our users. Continued usage of the BeautySpot platform entails that you agree to any changes in the updated policy.
